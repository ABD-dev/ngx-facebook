Like Button (class)

A single click on the Like button will 'like' pieces of content on the web and share them on Facebook. You can also display a Share button next to the Like button to let people add a personal message and customize who they share with.

Usage 
<fb-like href="https://www.facebook.com/zuck"></fb-like>
Inputs action (string) colorScheme (string) href (string) kidDirectedSite (boolean) layout (string) ref (string) share (boolean) showFaces (boolean) size (string) width (string) Outputs Instance Methods
